What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit websites. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies Required for the website to function properly. These cookies enable basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas. Session
Functionality Cookies Remember your preferences and choices (such as language preferences) to provide enhanced, more personal features. Up to 1 year
Analytics Cookies Help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. Up to 2 years
Performance Cookies Collect information about how visitors use our website to improve how the website works. Up to 1 year

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that place cookies on your device:

  • Google Analytics: For analyzing website traffic and user behavior
  • Google Fonts: For displaying custom fonts on our website
  • Font Awesome: For displaying icons

Important: Our notepad editor does not use cookies to store your customized designs. All customization is done locally in your browser.
